## What Happens When You Don't Have Privacy

### Competitor Intelligence
Your rivals see your payout volumes in real-time. They can estimate your revenue, identify your VIP players, and poach them with targeted offers. That $2M whale you just paid out? Your competitor knows about it within minutes and is already sliding into their DMs.

### Regulatory Exposure
US and EU authorities can trace funds back to your operation even if you're licensed in Curacao or Malta. They don't care about your license when they can see the money flow. One flagged transaction and you're explaining yourself to regulators who already have your entire transaction history mapped out.

### Player Targeting
Whales who receive large payouts become targets for hackers, phishing attacks, and social engineering. When a player's $500K withdrawal is public, they become a walking target. Your biggest customers become security liabilities.

### Banking Derisking
Banks see gambling-linked transactions and freeze accounts. They close relationships without warning. Even crypto-friendly banks are getting nervous about traceable gambling flows. Your payment rails disappear overnight.

### Tax Authority Scrutiny
Even in crypto-friendly jurisdictions, visible large payouts trigger audits. Tax authorities worldwide are getting sophisticated about blockchain analysis. Your "privacy-friendly" jurisdiction won't protect you from mathematical certainty.

### Blacklisting Cascade
One flagged address contaminates all connected wallets through taint analysis. Your entire hot wallet infrastructure gets marked as high-risk. Exchanges start rejecting your deposits. Your operational wallets become toxic.
